About Me

My name is Jack, or you may know me as Geb online.

I play games such as Skyrim, Elden Ring, Fallout, GTA, CS:GO, Overwatch, Minecraft, and Terraria.

That's not all I do though. I mod games, meaning I turn (single-player) experiences like Skyrim into my own.

I've been playing guitar since 2018, and I played ice hockey around the same time.

I'm a writer, working on various forms of poetry and nonfiction, as well as a large fiction fantasy world-building project.

I also love being outside, hiking in nature and taking photographs. I appreciate good food as well.

I like general decency among people. A willingness to accept humor and learn to laugh rather than cry. People who are compassionate and care for others. People who pursue their interests. People who are comfortable with themselves.
