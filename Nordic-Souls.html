Nordic Souls is a comprehensive visual and gameplay overhaul for Skyrim SE/AE designed to modernize the game while staying true to Skyrim's Nordic theme. It provides a perfect balance between a plenty of new content, a complete SimonRim gameplay overhaul, and beautiful yet performance-friendly visuals.

Despite the name, this list does not attempt to "turn Skyrim into Dark Souls." This list is focused on modernizing Skyrim, not turning the game into something else.

Everything about the game is improved, but still recognizably Skyrim. On top of a beautiful (and customizable!) graphics overhaul, tons of new quests have been added, loads of new gear is naturally integrated, and a lightweight gameplay overhaul improves mechanics while adding cool new features. No matter what, you'll always have something to do. Have fun!

Features

SimonRim suite of gameplay overhauls, including mods from other authors, for consistent gameplay
Quest mods like Wyrmstooth, Vigilant, Unslaad, The Forgotten City, Sirenroot, and more
Interesting NPCs and Citizens of Tamriel, and many other followers including Inigo, Lucien, and Auri
Rudy ENB for Obsidian Weathers as a baseline for the beautiful graphics overhaul
Extensive lore-friendly character customization options, with many presets included
Good performance on almost any machine
